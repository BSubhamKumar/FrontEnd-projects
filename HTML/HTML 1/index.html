Welcome to the Grievance redressal



                
                    The grievance procedure is a machinery to sort out the issues between student and college. It is a means by which a student
                    who believe that, he / she has been treated unfairly with respect to his / her academic / administrative affairs or is
                    convinced to be discriminated is redressed.

Types of Grievances :
  • Grievance related to Admission.

  • Grievance related to Victimization.

  • Grievance related to Attendance.

  • Grievance related to charging of fees.

  • Grievance regarding non-transparent or unfair evaluation process.

  • Non-observation of AICTE norms and standards.

  • Refusal to return documents such as certificates and more...
